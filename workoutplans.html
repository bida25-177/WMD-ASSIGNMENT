Beginner Plan

Perfect for new lifters

  • 3 workout days per week
  • Full body training
  • Focus on form and consistency
  • Compound exercises for faster growth
  • Rest: 60 to 90 seconds between sets

Best Exercises

Squats, Push-Ups, Bench Press, Rows, Shoulder Press, Deadlifts

Muscle Building Tips

Train with moderate weights, eat enough protein, and prioritize recovery. Beginners grow fastest by mastering the basics and staying consistent.

Intermediate Plan

For consistent gym-goers

  • 4 to 5 workout days per week
  • Push/Pull/Legs split
  • Progressive overload focus
  • Higher training volume
  • Rest: 45 to 75 seconds between sets

Best Exercises

Incline Bench, Pull-Ups, Barbell Squats, Romanian Deadlifts, Dumbbell Press

Muscle Building Tips

Increase weight gradually over time, track your workouts, and aim for 7 to 9 hours of sleep. Consistent overload is key for continued muscle growth.

Advanced Plan

High intensity muscle growth

  • 5 to 6 workout days per week
  • Advanced hypertrophy training
  • Heavy compound + isolation exercises
  • High volume and intensity techniques
  • Strategic deload and recovery weeks

Best Exercises

Heavy Squats, Weighted Pull-Ups, Bench Press, Deadlifts, Cable Flys, Bulgarian Split Squats

Muscle Building Tips

Focus on progressive overload, recovery, nutrition timing, and training close to failure. Advanced lifters need precision and consistency to maximize growth.
